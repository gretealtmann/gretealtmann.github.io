The Mayerling Cipher: An Imperial Secret

Marta Weiss Investigations, Book 1

Vienna, January 1889. Crown Prince Rudolf is dead. The official story has changed three times today.

Marta Weiss is a Jewish investigative journalist who receives an anonymous letter — three words and a fragment of a ciphered Imperial document. What she uncovers is worse than murder.

For readers of Kate Quinn and Allison Pataki: a razor-sharp historical thriller about the women history buries, and what happens when one of them refuses to stay down.

About the Author

Grete Altmann writes atmospheric historical mysteries set in the twilight years of the Habsburg Empire. A former archivist with a doctorate in Central European cultural history, she developed her affinity for Vienna's layered political underworld while researching the fin-de-siècle press corps.

Her fiction dwells in the tension between what the official record preserves and what it deliberately buries. The Mayerling Cipher is her debut novel.
